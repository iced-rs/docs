Creates an iced Daemon given its title, update, and view logic.

A Daemon will not open a window by default, but will run silently instead until a Task from window::open is returned by its update logic.

Furthermore, a Daemon will not stop running when all its windows are closed. In order to completely terminate a Daemon, its process must be interrupted or its update logic must produce a Task from exit.
